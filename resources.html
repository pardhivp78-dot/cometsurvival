Utilize What You Pay For

A major part of surviving your freshman year is recognizing that you don't have to do it alone. Your tuition pays for a massive network of support systems designed explicitly to help you succeed.

Never hesitate to book a session at the Student Success Center if you are falling behind in a class, or visit the Career Center to start building your resume early. The most successful students are the ones who ask for help when they need it.
