Bouncing Back & Staying Focused

University academics are a massive step up from high school, and it is completely normal to feel overwhelmed. One bad grade does not define your college career, but how you react to it does.

In this section, we break down how to effectively track your syllabus, the most efficient ways to manage your study time, and how to utilize campus resources to get back on track when you fall behind.

Mental Health

Your mental well-being is just as important as your GPA. Remember to take frequent breaks away from your screen. Do not hesitate to reach out to the UTD Student Counseling Center if you are feeling overwhelmed.
