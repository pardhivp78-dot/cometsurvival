Building Healthy Habits

The "Freshman 15" isn't just about weight; it is about the sudden shift in lifestyle when you are fully in charge of your own schedule, meals, and sleep habits.

This section is dedicated to helping you establish routines that keep your energy levels high. We cover how to navigate dining hall menus, the basics of cooking healthy meals in a dorm or apartment, and how to fit physical activity into a packed academic schedule.

Health Tip

Carry a reusable water bottle to every class. Staying hydrated is one of the easiest ways to fight off mid-afternoon fatigue and keep your brain sharp during long lectures!
